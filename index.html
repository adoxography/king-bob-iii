King Bob III

Introduction

This is King Bob III. He is a friendly little blob who is still learning.

Try saying some of these!

  • "What is your name?

  • "Do you like spaghetti?

  • "Knock knock!"

  • "Where is ___?" (Bob knows about Canadian capitals)

  • "Sing/say/rap the huggies commercial song/the vegetable song/the diaper rap/baby shark"

NOTE: King Bob III plays nicely with Google Chrome; he is not tested with other browsers. He will need access to your microphone and speakers to work.

*Note: it might take Bob a few seconds to say something the first time his voice is changed.
